Rules of Play / Instructions:

Whack-a-mole is a game of reflexes. Over the course of 30 seconds, moles will continually appear and disappear. Your mission, if you choose to accept it, is to whack as many moles as possible in that period of time.

  • Five moles will appear at a time,
    and disappear after a moment.
  • Moles that are "whacked" will explode.
  • Moles that are not whacked will disappear.
  • For each mole that you whack, you earn one point.
  • You always win!
    (If you hit zero moles, you win at being nice.)
